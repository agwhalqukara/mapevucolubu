    Wedding bells for the Browns! Sister Wives daughter Maddie Rose Brown is engaged to her boyfriend, Caleb Brush, TLC confirms to Us Weekly.

    “Happy to share this with you guys,” Maddie, 19, tweeted on Sunday, Sept. 13, alongside a link announcing her engagement news. She also shared some details about what to expect from her upcoming nuptials. “I showed Caleb the song I have wanted to walk down the [aisle] to since I was 15,” she wrote. “He got a little emotional when hearing it. So sweet!!”

    The star added that she will continue her education before marriage. “There have been a lot of questions on me finishing school. YES,” she tweeted on Monday. “I will go all the way through till I finish with my Law degree.” Maddie has oftentimes gushed about her future husband via social media. In July, she wrote on Instagram: “Thank you for making me laugh everyday and teaching me about the difference between a 30-06 and a 7mag. I kinda dig you.”

    TLC also shared additional details from Maddie’s engagement, and photos of her ring.

    “Love is in the air for Sister Wives‘ daughter Maddie who is newly engaged to fiance Caleb Brush!” the network confirmed in its post. “And, despite growing up in a polygamist family, Maddie and Caleb are opting to have a monogynous marriage.”

    According to the network, Brush asked Brown family patriarch, Kody Brown, for permission to wed his daughter. “With Kody’s blessing, Caleb surprised Maddie with a blue diamond ring at a scenic look-out point,” TLC revealed. “The happy couple look forward to planning an outdoor country wedding with family and friends.”

    Maddie is the daughter of Janelle Brown, one of Kody’s four wives including Meri, pregnant Robyn, and Christine Brown. While the Brown family patriarch and wives are best known for their polygamist lifestyle, Maddie said on Sunday that she wanted a monogamous marriage.

    “We are not living plural marriage,” Brush told People, which first broke the story. “We support Maddie’s family with their choice of living plural marriage and they support us with our choice in just marrying each other.”

    Her family also reacted to the news. “Congratulations @MaddieR_Brown I love you!…. and Caleb too!” her dad, Kody, tweeted after the announcement was made.

    The newly engaged reality star’s biological mother, Janelle, celebrated by retweeting Maddie’s announcement, while the other wives chimed in on the family news. “Congratulations @MaddieR_Brown!” Robyn wrote, while Christine elaborated: “Seriously?! They’re perfect together. Congratulations @MaddieR_Brown and Caleb.”
