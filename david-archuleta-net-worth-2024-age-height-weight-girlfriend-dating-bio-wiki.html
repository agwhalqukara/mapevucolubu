    Early life

    David Archuleta was born on 28th December 1990 in Miami. His mother Guadalupe Mayorga was a salsa singer and dancer and his father; Jeff Archuleta was a Jazz musician. David Archuleta had four siblings.

    His childhood was spent in Salt Lake City, Utah where he attended Murray High School. He graduated in 2009. In 2010 he volunteered for two years to work as a full- time missionary in Santiago, Chile.

    He also did his graduation in modelling and acting from Barbizon modelling and acting school.

    Age, Height, and Weight

    Being born on 28 December 1990, David Archuleta is 33 years old as of today’s date 22nd July 2024. His height is 1.68 m tall, and weight is 68 kg.

    Career

    In 2003 he participated in several episodes of TV show ‘Star Search’ and became a junior vocal champion on star search 2, eventually losing Grand champion title to Tiffany Evans. Around this time he found that he had partial vocal paralysis but did not opt for surgery. He dabbled with writing songs with a song ‘don’t tell me’.

    He came out with his first self-titled album in 2008. It had many amazing songs such as ‘A little too not over you, ‘Crush’, and ‘Touch my hand’. His prodigious singing talent is evident in these songs. This album debuted as no two on the billboard 200 chart. It sold 750000 in US and 900000 worldwide.

    In 2009 he came with his second album titled ‘Christmas from the heart’ in which he sang traditional songs and some songs written by him. His next album ‘The other side of down’ (Oct. 2010) had title song along with popular songs like ‘Elevator’, ‘stomping the roses’ and ‘who I am’.

    In his next album ‘Begin’ (Aug. 2012) he experiments with softer songs. His song ‘Be still’ became a rage. More albums like ‘forevermore’ and ‘No matter how far’ came, making it a new album every year from 2008 to 2013.

    His album ‘postcards in the sky’ released in Oct. 2017 depicts who he is as a person. Most of the songs in this album were written by him.
