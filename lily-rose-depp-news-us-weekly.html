    About

    Lily-Rose Depp was born in May 1999 to actor Johnny Depp and French model Vanessa Paradis. (Her parents, who also share son Jack, called it quits in 2012 after 14 years together.)

    The Pirates of the Caribbean actor’s eldest child made her acting debut in 2014 with a cameo in Tusk. Two years later, she starred in Yoga Hoser, The Dancer and Planetarium. Lily-Rose continued to make a name for herself at the box office with roles in 2018’s Savage, 2021’s Voyagers and Wolf. Her TV debut came with 2023’s The Idol, in which she plays the lead character, pop star Jocelyn.

    Lily-Rose is also a model and was named a Chanel brand ambassador in 2015. The following year, she became the face of the brand’s iconic perfume, Chanel No. 5 L’Eau.

    As for her personal life, the Silent Night actress made headlines in 2018 when she was first linked to Timothée Chalamet. The King costars dated for two years before calling it quits in 2020. Lily-Rose was later spotted kissing Austin Butler in August 2021 before confirming in May 2023 that she was seeing rapper 070 Shake (real name Danielle Balbuena).
