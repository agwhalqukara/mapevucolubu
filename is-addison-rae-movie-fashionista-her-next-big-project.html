    Addison Rae, a TikTok star, has confirmed her next major movie, starring in Paramount’s upcoming movie, Fashionista.

    Fans may remember her from her TikTok days, but she’s since gone on to become a Hollywood celebrity.

    Since He’s All That, this is her first official role. Addison Rae played a teen influencer who transforms a nerdy boy from her school into the prom king in the Netflix film. 

    However, after her film debut, the aspiring actor has ambitions to work on a variety of projects.

    Addison Rae’s movie, He’s All that, is her first Time performing on the Big screen. 

    She made her film debut in August 2021 in the Netflix Original ‘He’s All That.’ Since then, she’s talked a lot about what kind of movie she may be looking for.

    “Being an actress has always been a dream of mine,” the “Obsessed” singer confessed to Glamour U.K. in February 2021. “To be truly referred to as one now is amazing.” I was usually in acting courses as a kid, and I used to pretend to be the character in movies.”

    Addison Rae, TikTok’s favorite it-girl, is transferring her talents from our phones to the big screen once more.

    The TikTok sensation announced on Instagram on February 22, 2022, that she has signed on to star in ‘Fashionista,’ a new film produced by Paramount Players, a production imprint of Paramount Pictures that focuses on ‘current properties.’

    Addison Rae’s movie appears to have landed her next prominent studio feature, as she is scheduled to star in Fashionista from Paramount Players. 

    The final script was written by Laura Terruso, based on a draught by Amy Andelson and Emily Meyer. Ethea Entertainment’s Jennifer Gibgot and Andrew Panay are producing, and Ashley Brucks will oversee the Studio.

    The plotline is being kept a secret. While there is currently no director attached to the Addison Rae Movie, it is under active development, with a decision on a director expected in the coming weeks.

    Rae just inked a multi-million dollar Netflix deal that rivals any prominent A-total lister’s agreement with the streaming service.

    Given her global prominence as a result of her massive TikTok following of more than 80 million people, an increasing number of companies regard her as having an immediate impact on the initiatives they are working on.

    Nonetheless, even with her Netflix deal, Fashionista demonstrates that large studios are also eager to partner with Rae. 

    Several studios were competing for Rae’s attention after the success of He’s All That, according to insiders, but Fashionista was exactly the type of film she wanted to make next.
